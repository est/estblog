纪念Benoît B. Mandelbrot，1924-2010，分形几何之父

Posted 2010-10-16 | archive

Benoît B. Mandelbrot，the father of fractal geometry, has died at age 85 from pancreatic cancer. He passed away at a hospice in Cambridge, MA on October 14th

分形几何之父，本华·曼德博，由于胰腺癌于2010-10-14在Cambridge, MA一家医院以85岁高龄去世。R.I.P.
