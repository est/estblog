Never argue with an idiot ... or really intelligent fags

Posted 2009-12-16 | archive

记得TOP 100 funniest one-liners on the internet有这么一句精辟的话：

Never argue with an idiot, they'll only drag you down to their level then beat you by experience.

我今天算是领教到了，这个世界上还有另外一种极端情况，那就是特别有学问的fags。他们也会引导你远离argument本身而向他们熟知的领域，然后用权威你知识打败你。

通常情况fags会做这些事情，有人已经把这些行为很好的归类了：

  1. 偷换主题，aka 歪楼
  2. 文字游戏
  3. 索隐发微
  4. 故作高深

这种模式的一个问题就是 those really smart fags do not solve any actually problems neither get things done.

不过我又重新温习了一下 rules of the Internet，发现 #11 - #26已经很好的总结了这个情况了。学习啊学习。

11. All your carefully picked arguments can easily be ignored.
12. Anything you say can and will be used against you.
13. Anything you say can be turned into something else. - fixed
14. Do not argue with trolls, it means that they win.
15. The harder you try, the harder you will fail.
16. If you fail in epic proportions, it may just become a winning failure.
17. Every win fails eventually.
18. Everything that can be labeled, can be hated.
19. The more you hate it, the stronger it gets.
20. Nothing is to be taken seriously.
21. Original content is original for only a few seconds before getting old.
25. Relation to the original topic decreases with every single post.
26. Any topic can be turned into something totally unrelated.
